Hanoi Problem

  • There are three poles and N disks where each disk is heaver/bigger than the next disk. In the initial conguration, the discs are stacked upon another on the first pole where the lighter discs are above the heavier discs. We want to move all the discs to the last pole with the following conditions:
    • Only one disc can be moved from one pole to another at a time.
      一次只能将一个圆盘从一个极移动到另一个极。
    • The discs have to be stacked such that all the lighter discs are on top of the heavier ones.
      必须将光盘堆叠起来，以使所有较轻的光盘都放在较重的光盘上。

  • 解答思路
    To move N discs from the first pole to the last pole, we need to move N-1 discs to the middle pole, then move the Nth disc to the last pole, and then move all N-1 discs from the middle pole back to the last pole.
    要将N个圆盘从第一个极点移动到最后一个极点，我们需要将N-1圆盘移动到中间极点，然后将第N个圆盘移动到最后一个极点，然后将所有N-1圆盘从中间极点移回最后一个极点。
